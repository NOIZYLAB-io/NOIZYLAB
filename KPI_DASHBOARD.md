**NOIZYLAB KPI Dashboard (Operational Baselines)**

**Metrics**
- **Throughput (Storage/Network):** MB/s from speed tests (pre/post tuning).
- **Deploy Success Rate:** % successful `wrangler deploy` over attempts.
- **Incident Resolution Time:** Avg minutes from detection to resolution.
- **Rollback Frequency:** Count per month; goal: trending down.
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** Post-incident rating average.

**How to Record**
- Log lab outputs, timestamps, and environment details.
- Capture error messages and fixes for deploys.
- Note communication quality (structured updates used?).

**Targets**
- Throughput: increase vs baseline after tuning.
- Deploys: >95% success in stable phases.
- Incidents: reduce average time; fewer rollbacks.

**Review Cadence**
- Weekly standup review; monthly summary and trend analysis.
