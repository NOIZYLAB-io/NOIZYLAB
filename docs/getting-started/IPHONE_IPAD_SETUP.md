## ‚úÖ Method 1: Native Mail App (Recommended)

### iPhone Setup

#### Step 1: Add First Email Account
1. Open **Settings** app
2. Scroll down ‚Üí Tap **Mail**
3. Tap **Accounts**
4. Tap **Add Account...**
5. Select **Google** from the list
6. Enter your email: `rsp@noizylab.ca`
7. Tap **Next**
8. Enter your Google Workspace password
9. Complete two-factor authentication if prompted
10. Tap **Sign in**

#### Step 2: Configure Account Options
After signing in, you'll see account options:
- ‚úÖ **Mail** - Enable this
- ‚úÖ **Contacts** - Enable this
- ‚úÖ **Calendars** - Enable this
- ‚úÖ **Notes** - Optional (enable if you use Notes)
- ‚úÖ **Reminders** - Optional

Tap **Save** in top right corner

#### Step 3: Add Second Email Account
Repeat Steps 1-2 for:
- Email: `rp@fishmusicinc.com`

#### Step 4: Verify Settings
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Mail** ‚Üí **Accounts**
2. Tap on `rsp@noizylab.ca`
3. Tap **Account**
4. Verify settings:
   - **Description:** rsp@noizylab.ca (or customize)
   - **Email:** rsp@noizylab.ca
   - **Account:** Should show your full name

5. Tap **Advanced** (at bottom)
   - **IMAP Path Prefix:** `/`
   - **Use SSL:** ON
   - **Incoming Port:** `993`
   - **Outgoing Port:** `587` (or `465` for SSL)
   - **Use SSL:** ON

#### Step 5: Mail App Settings
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Mail**
2. Configure:
   - **Fetch New Data:** Push (recommended)
   - **Default Account:** Select your primary account
   - **Show To/Cc Label:** ON
   - **Load Remote Images:** Your preference
   - **Organize by Thread:** ON (recommended)

#### Step 6: Test Email
1. Open **Mail** app
2. Tap **Compose** (pencil icon, bottom right)
3. Send test email to yourself
4. Check it arrives in inbox
5. Send test email to external email (Gmail, Outlook)
6. Verify email arrives

**‚úÖ iPhone Mail App Setup Complete!**

## ‚öôÔ∏è Advanced Settings

### Mail App Advanced

1. **Settings** ‚Üí **Mail** ‚Üí **Accounts** ‚Üí Select account ‚Üí **Advanced**
   - **Mailbox Behaviors:**
     - **Drafts Mailbox:** [Gmail]/Drafts
     - **Sent Mailbox:** [Gmail]/Sent Mail
     - **Deleted Mailbox:** [Gmail]/Trash
     - **Archive Mailbox:** [Gmail]/All Mail
   
   - **IMAP Path Prefix:** `/`
   
   - **Incoming Settings:**
     - **Use SSL:** ON
     - **Authentication:** OAuth2
     - **Server Port:** `993`
   
   - **Outgoing Settings:**
     - **SMTP Server:** smtp.gmail.com
     - **Use SSL:** ON
     - **Authentication:** OAuth2
     - **Server Port:** `587` (or `465`)

### Troubleshooting

#### Email Not Syncing:
1. **Settings** ‚Üí **Mail** ‚Üí **Accounts** ‚Üí Select account
2. Turn account OFF, wait 10 seconds
3. Turn account ON
4. Wait for sync

#### Email Not Pushing:
1. **Settings** ‚Üí **Mail** ‚Üí **Accounts** ‚Üí **Fetch New Data**
2. Select **Push**
3. Verify account is set to **Push** (not Fetch)

#### Can't Send Email:
1. **Settings** ‚Üí **Mail** ‚Üí **Accounts** ‚Üí Select account ‚Üí **Account**
2. Check **Outgoing Mail Server** settings
3. Verify SMTP server: `smtp.gmail.com:587`

#### Calendar Not Syncing:
1. **Settings** ‚Üí **Calendar** ‚Üí **Accounts** ‚Üí Select account
2. Toggle **Calendars** OFF then ON
3. Open Calendar app, pull down to refresh
