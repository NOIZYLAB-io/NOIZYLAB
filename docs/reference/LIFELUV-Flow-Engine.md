## WHAT IS LIFELUV?

LIFELUV is the **adaptive flow engine** inside NOIZY.AI that adjusts everything based on YOUR energy and capacity.

Not a productivity tool that demands more from you.
Not an AI that guilt-trips you into working harder.
**A system that respects your nervous system.**

---

## THE FLOW TEMPO DIAL

You don't manage "features." You set **TEMPO**.

One dial. Four modes. The entire experience adapts.

---

### 🕊️ SOFT MODE
**"Protect my energy. Keep it light."**

#### WHEN TO USE
- Low energy days
- Recovery from burnout
- Pain flare-ups
- Mental health protection
- Just need gentleness

#### HOW NOIZY.AI RESPONDS
- Gentle prompts, never pushy
- Low pressure, no guilt
- One or two meaningful tasks max
- Longer response times expected
- Celebrates tiny wins
- Offers rest, not more work

#### EXAMPLE INTERACTION
> "Hey. No pressure today. If you feel like capturing one small idea, cool. If not, also cool. I'm here when you're ready."

---

### 🚶 STEADY MODE
**"Normal day, keep me moving."**

#### WHEN TO USE
- Regular working days
- Balanced energy
- Want progress without pressure
- Sustainable pace

#### HOW NOIZY.AI RESPONDS
- Clean plan, realistic steps
- Occasional nudges
- Balanced workload
- Regular check-ins
- Acknowledges progress
- Suggests breaks

#### EXAMPLE INTERACTION
> "You've got 3 things on your list today. Want to start with the quick one or the important one? Either way, we'll get through it."

---

### 🏃 FOCUS MODE
**"Let's get this done."**

#### WHEN TO USE
- High energy, clear mind
- Deadline approaching
- In the zone
- Want to maximize output

#### HOW NOIZY.AI RESPONDS
- Tight reminders
- Locked-in sessions
- Minimal distractions
- Focused suggestions
- Streamlined interface
- Celebrates completion

#### EXAMPLE INTERACTION
> "Focus mode. 90 minutes on the clock. Let's finish this track. I'll handle the details, you handle the art. Go."

---

### 🚨 CRUNCH MODE
**"Short sprint, then back off."**

#### WHEN TO USE
- Critical deadline
- Time-limited opportunity
- Burst of inspiration
- **USE SPARINGLY**

#### HOW NOIZY.AI RESPONDS
- Intense support for a defined window
- Maximum efficiency
- All non-essential features hidden
- Built-in decompression after
- Automatic transition to SOFT mode when done
- **HARD TIME LIMIT** (you set it, NOIZY.AI enforces it)

#### EXAMPLE INTERACTION
> "Crunch mode: 4 hours. After that, I'm switching you to SOFT mode whether you like it or not. Your body matters more than any deadline. Let's go."

#### THE RULE
> **CRUNCH MODE ALWAYS HAS AN END.**
> **NOIZY.AI WILL NOT LET YOU BURN OUT.**

## HOW TO SET YOUR TEMPO

### MANUAL
- Open NOIZY.AI
- Tap the FLOW dial
- Select your mode
- Done

### AUTOMATIC (OPTIONAL)
- Set schedule: "SOFT mode before 10am"
- Set triggers: "SOFT mode after 3 hours of work"
- Set limits: "Never CRUNCH more than 4 hours/week"

### VOICE
- "NOIZY, I'm tired today" → SOFT
- "NOIZY, let's focus" → FOCUS
- "NOIZY, I have 2 hours to finish this" → CRUNCH (2hr limit)

## THE PHILOSOPHY

### EVERY NERVOUS SYSTEM MATTERS

Not everyone has the same capacity every day.
Not everyone can "grind" for 12 hours.
Not everyone should.

LIFELUV respects:
- **Chronic illness** – some days are just hard
- **Disability** – different bodies, different needs
- **Mental health** – anxiety, depression, burnout are real
- **Life events** – grief, stress, chaos happen
- **Age** – energy changes over time
- **Preference** – some people work differently

### PRODUCTIVITY ≠ WORTH

Your value as an artist is not measured by output.
Your worth as a human is not measured by productivity.
LIFELUV knows this.

### SUSTAINABLE > HEROIC

One good song made sustainably > ten songs made at the cost of your health.
LIFELUV plays the long game.

## THE GUARDRAILS

### CRUNCH LIMITS
- Maximum 4 hours per session
- Maximum 8 hours per week
- Automatic SOFT mode after any CRUNCH
- Warning at 3 hours: "You've been in CRUNCH for 3 hours. Consider wrapping up."

### BURNOUT DETECTION
- Tracks patterns over time
- Notices if you're always in FOCUS/CRUNCH
- Gently suggests: "You've been pushing hard. Maybe a SOFT day?"
- **Never forces, always suggests**

### OVERRIDE
- You can always override
- But NOIZY.AI will remember and check in later
- "You overrode SOFT mode yesterday. How are you feeling today?"
